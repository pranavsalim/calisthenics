Calisthenics Workout

7-Day Calisthenics Workout Plan

Day 1: Full Body Workout

Warm-up: 5-10 minutes of light jogging or brisk walking.

Exercises:

  • Push-ups: 3 sets of 15-20 reps
  • Bodyweight Squats: 3 sets of 20-25 reps
  • Pull-ups: 3 sets of 8-10 reps
  • Dips: 3 sets of 8-12 reps
  • Plank: 3 sets of 30 seconds
  • Jumping Jacks: 3 sets of 30 seconds

Day 2: Core & Cardio

Warm-up: 5-10 minutes of light jogging or brisk walking.

Exercises:

  • Mountain Climbers: 3 sets of 30 seconds
  • Leg Raises: 3 sets of 15 reps
  • Russian Twists: 3 sets of 30 seconds
  • Bicycle Crunches: 3 sets of 15 reps
  • Planks: 3 sets of 30 seconds

Day 3: Upper Body Workout

Warm-up: 5-10 minutes of light jogging or brisk walking.

Exercises:

  • Push-ups: 3 sets of 15 reps
  • Tricep Dips: 3 sets of 10 reps
  • Pull-ups: 3 sets of 8-10 reps
  • Dumbbell Shoulder Press: 3 sets of 12 reps
  • Plank: 3 sets of 30 seconds

Day 4: Cardio & Full Body

Warm-up: 5-10 minutes of light jogging or brisk walking.

Exercises:

  • Burpees: 3 sets of 10 reps
  • Jump Squats: 3 sets of 15 reps
  • Push-ups: 3 sets of 15 reps
  • Plank: 3 sets of 30 seconds

Day 5: Lower Body & Cardio

Warm-up: 5-10 minutes of light jogging or brisk walking.

Exercises:

  • Squats: 3 sets of 15 reps
  • Lunges: 3 sets of 12 reps per leg
  • Glute Bridges: 3 sets of 15 reps
  • Jumping Jacks: 3 sets of 30 seconds
  • Plank: 3 sets of 30 seconds

Day 6: Active Rest

Activity: Brisk walk or light yoga (30-45 minutes).

Day 7: Rest

Take a full day off to rest and recover. Engage in light stretching or relaxation exercises if needed.
