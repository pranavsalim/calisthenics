7-Day Calisthenics Workout Plan

Track your progress and stay fit with this structured weekly plan.

Day 1 - Push Day

  • Push-ups: 3 sets of 15 reps
  • Incline Push-ups: 3 sets of 12 reps
  • Dips: 3 sets of 10 reps

Day 2 - Pull Day

  • Pull-ups: 3 sets of 10 reps
  • Chin-ups: 3 sets of 10 reps
  • Bodyweight Rows: 3 sets of 12 reps

Day 3 - Core

  • Plank: 3 sets of 1 min
  • Leg Raises: 3 sets of 15 reps
  • Russian Twists: 3 sets of 20 reps

Day 4 - Rest

Take a rest day to let your muscles recover.

Day 5 - Leg Day

  • Squats: 3 sets of 15 reps
  • Lunges: 3 sets of 12 reps each leg
  • Calf Raises: 3 sets of 20 reps

Day 6 - Full Body

  • Burpees: 3 sets of 10 reps
  • Push-ups: 3 sets of 15 reps
  • Pull-ups: 3 sets of 10 reps

Day 7 - Active Recovery

Go for a light jog or stretch to improve flexibility.

Stay consistent and achieve your fitness goals!
