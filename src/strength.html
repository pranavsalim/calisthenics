Strength Training - Calisthenics

Weighted Pull-Ups

Weighted pull-ups build serious upper body strength, targeting the back, arms, and shoulders.

Pistol Squats

Pistol squats are a great exercise for building leg strength and balance using only bodyweight.

Handstand Push-Ups

Handstand push-ups are an advanced bodyweight exercise for developing shoulder and tricep strength.

Dip Bar Leg Raises

Dip bar leg raises target the core and hip flexors while engaging the upper body for stability.
